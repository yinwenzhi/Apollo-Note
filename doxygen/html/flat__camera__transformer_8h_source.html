17 /* Transform objects in 3D camera space into 3D ego-car space
18  *
19  * Two assumptions are used for this module
20  *
21  * 1. The ego-car space is on flat ground. 3D objects touches the ground
22  * (Z direction of this local space is always vertical to ground plane,
23  * regardless of ego car pose)
24  *
25  * 2. The input 3D distances for objects, from camera origin to object center,
26  * is accurate and in unit meter
27  */
